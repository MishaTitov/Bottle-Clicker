> Click on Bottle to collect a bottle
  - Bottle per Click = BpC
> Buy Buildings to collect bottle each second
  - Bottle per Second = BpS
> Buy Upgrades to upgrade you Buildings, BpC, BpS and etc.
> Randomizer each 2min random drops 1 of 4 game effect:
  - 'Luck' gives you 10% of amount bottles
  - 'Fail' remove 5% of amount bottles
  - 'Buff BpC' gives +777% BpS to BpC for 17sec
  - 'Buff BpS' gives +7% BpS to BpS and BpC for 77sec
> Time Upgrade gives you +N% BpS to BpS and BpC for each Nth hour in game
> Each 5 Achievements gives you +N% BpS To BpS and BpC
  - N = (unlocked achievements) / 5
